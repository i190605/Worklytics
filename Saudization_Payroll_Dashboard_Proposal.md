## 1) Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the delivery of a **Saudization-Aware Payroll Dashboard** designed for organizations operating in Saudi Arabia. The solution combines payroll visibility with Saudization compliance tracking, enabling leadership, HR, and payroll teams to make faster and safer workforce decisions.

The dashboard will replace manual spreadsheet workflows with an interactive web interface that provides real-time KPIs, department-level compliance insights, hiring recommendations, and exportable reports.

---

## 2) Business Problem

Most organizations currently track Saudization and payroll data across disconnected sheets and reports. This creates:

- Delayed decision-making
- High risk of reporting errors
- Limited visibility into non-compliant departments
- Difficulty forecasting hiring needs to meet Saudization targets
- Weak management-level reporting and audit readiness

---

## 3) Proposed Solution

We propose a browser-based dashboard with the following capabilities:

### Core Dashboard Features
- Workforce KPIs (Total Employees, Saudi Employees, Non-Saudi Employees)
- Saudization % (overall and department-level)
- Monthly payroll overview
- Open vacancy tracking

### Interactive Controls
- Search by department
- Filter by compliance status (All / Compliant / At Risk)
- Sort by rate, name, or vacancy volume
- Toggle to show only departments with open vacancies
- Adjustable Saudization target slider (e.g., 30%–90%)

### Intelligence & Actions
- Department-level compliance status badge
- Automatic “recommended Saudi hires” per department
- Compliance insights panel with target gap indicators
- CSV export for monthly reports and internal reviews

### Odoo-Inspired HR Operations Enhancements
- Approval Workflow for **Leave Requests** and **Payroll Exceptions**
- Approve/Reject actions from a centralized Approvals queue
- Priority and status tracking for faster operational closure
